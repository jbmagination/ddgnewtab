About

This is an extension that gives you a new tab that is based around DuckDuckGo. It also contains a date and time clock at hand. Users who like DuckDuckGo should definitely give this one a try. You can search DuckDuckGo directly on the page, and you can see the date & time as well. There's also a GitHub repository for people who like open-source projects, visible in the Downloads section.
